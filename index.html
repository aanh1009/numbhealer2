How Numerology Affects Our Lives

Numerology suggests that each number holds specific vibrations and energies. These vibrations influence various aspects of our lives, including personality traits, relationships, career choices, and life events. Numerology provides insights into our strengths, weaknesses, and potential opportunities. By understanding the numeric influences in our lives, we can make informed decisions, align ourselves with favorable vibrations, and tap into our true potential. It allows us to gain a deeper understanding of ourselves and our life's purpose, leading to personal growth, fulfillment, and self-realization.

MY OWN STORY

MY BACKGROUND

My name is Anh, I am a Vietnammese living in Ho Chi Minh City. Since 2021, I have been going to a prestigious gifted school, majoring in Mathematics.  

It was 4 years ago when my life started to diverge.  My parents made a fuss over each other with the smallest details. Ever since, I have incurred an obtrusive sense of unworthiness and self-insuffiency. I embarked on eating everything I could, no matter hungry or not, for the naive belief that the food would fill the vacancy in my heart, not my stomach. 

MY OWN STORY

THE TURNING POINT

At the end of the first semester in 11th grade, my situation got worse as the blood test indicated a cautious level of cholesterol. I was determined to control my weight

That was when I came across a Numerology book at my sister's house. Initially, I did not dare to take a leap of faith, as I have been spending my life coining such things "pseudoscience". But as the book itself was  pageturner,  I kept progressing until I hit the section "Heal with your numbers".

MY OWN STORY

the ending - the renewal

After discovering my susceptibility to eating disorder as my Life Path Number was 9, I embarked on employing the practice of Manifestation. "I am worthy of love and I am sufficient on my own". That was what repeated every morning when I woke up for 5 months, until I officially lost 4 kilograms.

Inspired by the book and exhilarated by my own journey, I commenced a new journey. Although it was hard at times as I was a completely newcomer to coding, I never gave up. And today, I can proudly introduce myself as the founder of this website -"The Numbhealer".
